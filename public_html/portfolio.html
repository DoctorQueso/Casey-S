PORTFOLIO AND PROJECTS

Click the graphics to learn more about each project.

NATURALIST

Naturalist is a Minecraft mod built in Java that I helped in development, ideating, and a little bit of art. Over the lifetime of the mod, it has garnished a little over 15 million downloads.

ANOTHER FURNITURE MOD

Another Furniture Mod is a Minecraft mod built in Java that aims to complete the users design experience within the game by adding a multitude of different furniture items, and other blocks. I aided in the ideation, creation, and management of the mod.

OMNIS

Omnis is a java creation for Minecraft that I co-developed and designed entirely from the ground up, boasting 4.5M+ downloads. It has made its way into youtube videos, modpacks, and all the way around the Minecraft community.

SKYBLOCK SHOWDOWN

I helped project manage, and administrate this event in partnership with Xbox and Verizon (produced by Boom.tv) in just a few months. Our team (me, my bosses, and one programmer) were able to successfully create a smooth-running project for several popular content creators.

WEAPONS UNLIMITED

Weapons Unlimited is a world that I helped design a couple of years ago. It is released on the Minecraft Marketplace. I was in charge of designing the UI, alongside some 3D models and a bunch of 2D item textures. This was one of my first projects under my studio, and it was before I worked into a managerial position.

THREE THOUSAND WEAPONS

3000 Weapons was among one of the first projects I helped manage for. I was in charge of a majority of the testing, reporting any issues or bugs I found, as well as. I was in constant communication with Microsoft in order to ensure that we were to comply within their rules.
